## Strategic Observations

- 99.901% answer rate and 30s time-to-agent are strong service quality metrics -- the Contact Center is operationally reliable
- ~11-12% average resolution rate means roughly 88% of inbound calls require a transfer -- this is the core inefficiency the guided access model addresses
- September resolution rate drops to 6.93% -- likely semester-start volume surge overwhelming routing capacity
- 13,884 students registered after outbound contact = direct enrollment impact, quantifiable
- "Undecided about returning" (26.9%) + "Time away from school" (26.0%) together represent over half of non-attending students -- prime candidates for persistence campaigns
- 5,936 students unable to be contacted outbound = a real population being missed; worth segmenting for alternative channel outreach (SMS, Brutus)
