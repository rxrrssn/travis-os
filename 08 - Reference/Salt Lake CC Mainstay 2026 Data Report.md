## Gen AI Feature Overview

Enabled at SLCC starting Fall 2025 (no additional cost per Mainstay).

Features enabled:
- **Generative AI in Knowledge Base** -- responses trained on institution websites and docs; handles questions that match an understanding
- **Flash Responses** -- generates AI responses for questions with NO match in the Knowledge Base (fallback handler)
- **Live Chat response suggestions** -- contextual suggestions to staff during live chat
- **Knowledge Base response suggestions** -- AI assistant when writing KB entries
- **Fuzzy question generation** -- generates understanding variations to improve match accuracy
- **Contact conversation summaries** -- auto-generated summary displayed on contact profile
- **Escalation conversation summaries**

Available but not confirmed enabled at SLCC:
- Scripts: gen-AI discussions
- Scripts: gen-AI voice calls

## Strategic Observations

- **Gen AI impact is dramatic and real**: match rate jumped from 61% to 89% SP25 to SP26, missed questions dropped from 5,261 to 49. The bot is now substantively more capable without additional cost.
- **26% after-hours usage** confirms Brutus provides student access that staff cannot. This is a meaningful 24/7 value proposition that is easy to defend institutionally.
- **Academics (31%) and Admissions (8%)** dominate question categories -- registration-related questions are the primary driver. Aligns with the Contact Center's inbound routing patterns.
- **SMS reach is consistently 4-8x web reach**: 30-37k SMS unique students vs 3-7k web. Students are on SMS, not web chat.
- **SP26 active engagement dropped to 12.9%** (from ~20% in SU25-FA25). Worth monitoring -- could reflect campaign mix, list fatigue, or seasonal patterns.
- **Response rates have declined over time** (10.7% SP24 → 5.8% SP26). Passive engagement (open/delivery) is stable, but two-way conversation rates are falling. Relevant to One Voice strategy -- students may be consuming Brutus as a push channel more than a dialogue channel.
