# OSC White Paper -- Fall 2025

Source: `08 - Reference/OSC White Paper Fall 2025.pdf`

Author: Travis Hornbuckle. Data provided by the OSC team.

Period: Fall 2025

Note: Full document may contain additional pages beyond what was ingested (pdftoppm unavailable for extended read).

---

## Purpose Statement

The Online Success Coaching (OSC) program provides personalized, proactive support to SLCC's online learners, helping them navigate challenges and achieve their goals.

Through holistic, student-centered coaching, OSC fosters student persistence, academic success, and personal empowerment through high-touch, data-informed, and relationship-based coaching that connects students to resources, builds resilience, and supports individualized goal achievement.

---

## SLCC Vision Matrix Alignment

### ENGAGE
- **"High touch to resources"**
  - % of proactive cohort outreach (prior to and first 3 weeks of semester)
  - # of specific, timely outreach (calls, texts, emails)
- **"Holistic, student-centered coaching"**
  - # of evidence-based and best practice coaching methodologies, training, conferences, cross-departmental collaborations, bi-weekly resource sharing

### COMPLETE
- **"Data-informed resilience building"**
  - % of Canvas activity/engagement and assessment data
  - % persistence rates semester to semester
- **"Relationship-based coaching to support individualized goals"**
  - # and % of monthly online check-ins and semester survey assessment tools

---

## Program Objectives

Students who connect at least 3 times in a semester with OSC will:

1. Identify, navigate, and connect with in-time college resources through monthly phone calls, texts, and email
2. Reflect, self-assess, and build academic skills and confidence to determine S.M.A.R.T. semester goals
3. Communicate and collaborate with their coach through outreach and monthly check-in forms
4. Persist into the next semester and increase academic performance capacity through Canvas course engagement and semester surveys

---

## Dashboard Vision

Proposed dashboards per objective:
- Outreach totals: calls, emails, texts, appointments, resources shared by type
- Goals set with coach and progress toward those goals
- Canvas engagement percentages and monthly check-in visualization
- Persistence rates and survey data visualization

---

## Strategic Observations

- The 3-touch threshold as the persistence trigger is a measurable, actionable metric -- easy to track and report
- Vision Matrix alignment is deliberate positioning for the post-HB 265 accountability environment
- The dashboard vision maps directly to TDX orchestration and Power BI reporting capabilities Travis already manages
- "Data-informed resilience building" framing elevates coaching from a soft service to a measurable institutional outcome
