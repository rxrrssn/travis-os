# Online Success Coaching

## What It Is

Supports persistence and engagement for online students at SLCC. Established Fall 2016 within eLearning department; now under Student Affairs / Contact Center umbrella (transitioning to Bruin Connect -> Coaching).

Functions as: advising, student success coaching, retention operations, outreach coordination, and Care Network triage.

Common activities:
- Proactive outreach (phone, text, email, Canvas)
- Intervention tracking via MySuccess
- Early alert flag management (for full student population, not just OSC cohorts)
- Engagement support through Canvas OSC course
- Accountability follow-up
- Campaign execution (Strong Start pre-semester campaign)
- Registration support and next-semester persistence

## Scale

- ~5,400 students served annually
- ~400 students per coach per semester (evolved from 125 in 2016)
- 5 coaches + AD (Megan Brandly) + Director (Seini Pahulu, 30% allocation to OSC)
- Fully remote team

## Target Population

Assigned cohort criteria (one or more):
- Enrolled in at least 2 online courses
- Fewer than 15 completed credits
- Academically at-risk: probation, first-generation, new to college, condensed 8-week/half-term courses

## Current Systems

- MySuccess (Starfish by Ellucian) -- case management, early alerts, appointments, cross-departmental notes
- Transitioning toward Navigate360 (risk: reduced tracking nuance vs Starfish)
- Outreach Tracker (internal spreadsheet, updated daily)
- Grade Tracker (BA-1125 Canvas Weekly Report, built with DSA) -- daily grade data
  - Emerging Learners: score <= 60% AND no Canvas login for 5+ days
  - Star Students: one+ course >= 90% AND no courses below 70%
- Canvas OSC Course -- onboarding, Motivational Mondays (automated weekly), monthly check-in forms
- Mainstay for outreach campaigns
- TDX for workflow and interaction tracking
- Power BI for operational reporting

## Measurement Framework

Aligned to SLCC Vision Matrix under ENGAGE and COMPLETE.

Key threshold: 3 coaching connections per semester is the persistence trigger (98% vs 61%).

Tracked metrics:
- % proactive cohort outreach (prior to and first 3 weeks of semester)
- # timely outreach contacts (calls, texts, emails)
- % Canvas activity and assessment engagement
- % semester-to-semester persistence rates
- # monthly check-ins and survey completions
- MySuccess flag resolution rate (currently 95.9%)

Dashboard vision: one dashboard per program objective, covering outreach totals, goal-setting progress, Canvas engagement, and persistence rates. Maps to TDX + Power BI infrastructure.

## Key Challenges

- Navigate360 transition may reduce nuanced tracking previously available in Starfish
- Survey participation is low -- limits quality of student satisfaction data
- Workflow continuity at risk during platform transition

## Strategic Direction (as of SP26)

- Monitoring third-week churn and first-week Canvas login as early risk indicators
- Moving toward campus-wide open coaching (not just assigned cohorts) -- referral system active
- Improved survey participation strategy needed
- Program positioning: not just support service, but frontline enrollment intelligence (coaches surface stop-out signals early)
