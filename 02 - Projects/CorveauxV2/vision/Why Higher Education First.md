# Why Higher Education First

Higher education is not the final market.

It is the proving ground.

---

## Institutional Complexity

Few environments contain:

- Governance
- Identity
- Knowledge
- Communication
- Compliance
- Services
- Relationships
- Time

at the scale found within higher education.

The institution is both highly structured and highly decentralized.

This creates an ideal environment for validating the Corveaux model.

---

## Mission Alignment

Higher education exists to improve lives.

The mission is inherently human.

The consequences of fragmentation are visible.

Students experience the impact directly.

---

## Public Knowledge

Higher education institutions publish enormous amounts of information about themselves.

Programs.

Policies.

Departments.

Services.

Catalogs.

Directories.

Knowledge already exists.

It simply lacks structure.

---

## Validation Environment

If Corveaux can successfully model a college or university, it can likely model many other forms of institutions.

Higher education is the stress test.

---

## Final Principle

Higher education is where Corveaux proves the model.

It is not where the model ends.
