# What Corveaux Will Not Become

Vision documents explain what a company intends to become.

Equally important is understanding what a company must resist becoming.

As Corveaux grows, there will be opportunities to pursue adjacent markets, build disconnected features, and respond to competitive pressure. Not every opportunity should be pursued.

The purpose of this document is to define the boundaries that protect the integrity of the platform.

## Corveaux Will Not Become A Generic CRM

Corveaux may model relationships.

Corveaux may support engagement.

Corveaux may eventually provide capabilities commonly associated with CRM systems.

However, Corveaux does not exist to manage contacts.

Corveaux exists to model institutions.

Relationships are one component of the model, not the model itself.

---

## Corveaux Will Not Become A Generic CMS

Content is a projection.

The website is a projection.

The catalog is a projection.

The portal is a projection.

Corveaux does not exist to publish content.

Corveaux exists to understand what content should exist in the first place.

---

## Corveaux Will Not Become A Collection Of Disconnected Modules

Every capability must emerge from the canonical institutional model.

Features should not exist simply because customers request them.

Capabilities should exist because they strengthen institutional understanding.

Disconnected functionality creates the same fragmentation Corveaux was created to eliminate.

---

## Corveaux Will Not Become A Consulting Company

Consulting may accelerate adoption.

Consulting may support implementation.

Consulting may provide institutional expertise.

Consulting is not the product.

The product is the platform.

Corveaux succeeds when institutions become more capable, not when institutions become dependent.

---

## Corveaux Will Not Build For Competitive Parity

The existence of a feature in another product is not justification for building it.

Every capability must answer a single question:

Does this strengthen the institution's ability to understand, govern, or operate itself?

If not, it should be questioned.

---

## Final Principle

Corveaux does not exist to accumulate features.

Corveaux exists to reduce institutional complexity.

If a decision increases complexity without increasing institutional understanding, it is likely the wrong decision.
