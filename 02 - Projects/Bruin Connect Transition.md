# Bruin Connect Transition

## Context

Following Utah HB 265 Phase 1, academic affairs underwent budget cuts and program closures. Leadership now has heightened focus on demonstrating metrics from student-facing teams -- OSC and the Contact Center specifically.

The institution is simultaneously evaluating a structural shift in how students access support.

## The Problem

Current model: open access. Students call whoever they think is right. Results in:
- Fragmented departmental numbers across slcc.edu
- In-person enrollment management teams (admissions, financial aid, registrar, advising) fielding phone calls while trying to serve students physically present
- High transfer depth and IVR overhead institution-wide

## The Shift: Open Access -> Guided Access

Department phone numbers are being delisted from slcc.edu.

The Contact Center number (801-957-4073) will replace them as the single institutional contact point.

Students call one number. The team triages, assists, and routes -- giving enrollment management teams space to work with in-person students.

## Analysis Already Completed

**Contact Center direct inbound (07/01/2024 - 06/30/2025):**
- 48,416 calls received
- 99.901% answer rate
- 1:38 average call length
- ~11-12% monthly resolution rate (88% still require transfer out)

**Broader institutional IVR/call flow analysis (trailing 365 days, pulled late March/early April 2026):**
- ~85,000 calls across all institutional departments
- 3.2 average transfer depth
- 24 seconds average IVR menu time
- ~2,500 hours of student time lost to IVR/hold/transfer overhead

Same scope (Contact Center), different time windows. The 85k is the active figure used in the IVR analysis -- more current and covering a higher-volume period.

## Department Restructure

### Current Structure (umbrella: "Contact Center")
- Contact Center -- inbound team
- Contact Center -- outbound team
- Success Operations (Travis + admin assistant)
- Online Success Coaching (AD + 5 coaches)

### Proposed Structure (umbrella: "Bruin Connect")
- **Assist** -- replaces inbound Contact Center
- **Persist** -- replaces outbound Contact Center
- **Coaching** -- Online Success Coaching

### Rationale for Rename

"Contact Center" no longer reflects what the department actually does. The operation functions more as triage, persistence, and coaching than a traditional call center. "Bruin Connect" better represents the full scope and signals the institutional shift to students and staff.
