## Auto-stacked CompTIA credentials

Issued automatically once the prerequisite exams pass — no extra work, no extra cost:

- **IT Operations Specialist** = A+ (Core 1+2) + Network+
- **Secure Infrastructure Specialist** = A+ + Network+ + Security+
- **Secure Cloud Professional** = Cloud+ + Security+
- **Cloud Admin Professional** = Cloud+ + Network+

→ Finishing the BSIT nets roughly **8 individual certs + 4 stacks**.

## Not gating the BSIT

- **CompTIA Project+** — not offered in this program. E015 Project Management is a 3-task PA.
- **CAPM (PMI)** — optional, WGU voucher, not required to graduate; deferrable.
- **PSM I (Scrum.org)** — preferred first Scrum cert (lifetime, no renewal); natural add-on after E007/E015.
