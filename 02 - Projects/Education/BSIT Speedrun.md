# BSIT Speedrun

The plan to finish the BSIT as fast as WGU's model allows, then roll straight into the MSIT on Dec 1. See [[Education]] for live status, [[Course Log]] for course detail, [[Difficulty Calibration]] for the effort estimates driving this.

## The mechanic

- WGU is **flat tuition per 6-month term, unlimited courses.** Speed converts directly into time and money saved.
- Courses come in the **degree-plan order**, but I can request **up to 3 at a time** — always the next 3 in the list, workable in **any order within that window.**
- **Play:** lead each trio with its cert-gated or hardest course, so a proctor booking or a 14-day retake runs in the background while the other two get cleared. Keep at least one course always finishing to slide the window forward. Can't pull the 4th until one of the current 3 closes.

## The only real risk: three cert chokepoints

In 3-at-a-time mode a failed cert-gated course stalls everything behind it for the 14-day CompTIA retake wait. The three to over-prepare and first-try:

| Course | Cert | Why it's the risk |
|---|---|---|
| D318 Cloud Applications | **CompTIA Cloud+** | First chokepoint reached; 1/2 cloud diagnostic |
| D329 Network & Security – Applications | **CompTIA Security+** | 1/2 security diagnostic; sit after Network+ |
| D282 Cloud Foundations | **AWS Cloud Practitioner** | 1/2 cloud diagnostic |

Everything else calibrated Easy/Medium-for-me — coast those, spend the study energy here.
