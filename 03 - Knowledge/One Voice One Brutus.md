# One Voice One Brutus

## Core Idea

Students should experience a single institutional communication identity across the entire lifecycle.

## Problem

Current communication systems fragment identity across departments and platforms.

Examples:
- Recruitment messaging
- Advising outreach
- Retention campaigns
- Chatbot interactions

This fragmentation creates:
- trust erosion
- duplicated messaging
- inconsistent institutional tone
- disconnected lifecycle experiences

## Proposed Direction

Establish Brutus as the unified orchestration and engagement identity layer.

## Implications

- Governance required
- Shared communication standards
- Canonical student identity needed
- Cross-system orchestration layer required
