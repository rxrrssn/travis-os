# HB 265

## What It Is

Utah House Bill 265 -- higher education legislation driving budget and program restructuring across Utah institutions including SLCC.

## Phase 1 Impact at SLCC

Academic affairs underwent budget cuts and program closures. In the aftermath, leadership has heightened focus on:
- Demonstrating measurable outcomes from student-facing teams
- Operational efficiency
- Justifying departmental resources with metrics

## Relevance to Current Work

The post-HB 265 environment is a direct driver of the [[Bruin Connect Transition]]. Leadership needs to be able to point to what the [[Contact Center]] and [[Online Success Coaching]] are delivering. This creates both pressure and opportunity -- teams that can speak to metrics get protected and expanded.
